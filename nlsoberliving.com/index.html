NEW LIFE SOBER LIVING

by Legacy Management LLC

Sober Living in Draper and Taylorsville Utah. We are committed to providing a safe, drug and alcohol free environment for people dealing with addiction to recover. Our sober homes are a place to thrive and bloom in your recovery while transitioning back into mainstream society. Our clients come from every walk of life from business owners to students, we are here to meet you where you are and grow with you.

Our Sober Houses are centered around the 12 step recovery model. Weekly house meeting attendance is required as well as attendance of outside recovery meetings.
